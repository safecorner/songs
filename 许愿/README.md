
## English
```
[Verse 1]
In days of old, to build a桶 (wooden pail),
You needed carpentry skills without fail.
To forge a cart, years of experience required,
To write an App, coding mastery desired.
The old saying rang, heavy and deep:
"No diamond drill, no porcelain keep."
"Young man," they warned, with a frown and a look,
"Don't reach for the sky, don't shake the book!"
[Verse 2]
But now the drill is not yours to hold,
Just call the AI, brave and bold.
You craft the Spec, clear and bright,
Then tell the AI: "Make it right!"
No need for years in the workshop dust,
Just clear instructions, in them we trust.
[Chorus]
It's Spec-Driven Coding, like a wish to a Genie so free,
Who can't make a wish? Just you and me!
Write down the rules, let the magic start,
The Genie's ready to play its part.
Who can't make a wish? It's easy to do,
Just tell the Genie what dreams come true!
[Outro]
The Genie is waiting, the lamp is clean,
Young man, now you can dream unseen.
Reach for the sky...
The world is your machine.
```
